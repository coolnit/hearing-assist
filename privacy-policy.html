Privacy Policy for Hearing Assist

Last Updated: November 26, 2025

1. Introduction

Hearing Assist ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We collect minimal personal information:

  • Email Address: Required for user registration and account management
  • Account Credentials: Stored locally on your device for authentication

2.2 Audio Data

Important: We do NOT collect, store, or transmit your audio recordings or transcribed text. All speech recognition processing happens locally on your device using your device's built-in speech recognition capabilities.

2.3 Technical Information

We do not collect analytics, usage data, or device information.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your email address is used solely for:

  • User authentication and account management
  • Providing access to the app's features

4. Data Storage

All user data is stored locally on your device. We do not maintain servers or databases that store your personal information or transcription data.

5. Permissions

5.1 Microphone Access (RECORD_AUDIO)

We request microphone permission to:

  • Capture audio for real-time speech-to-text transcription
  • Enable the core functionality of the app

Your audio is processed locally and never transmitted or stored.

6. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit your data to third parties. Your information stays on your device.

7. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your information depends on your device's security measures. We recommend:

  • Using device lock screens
  • Keeping your device's operating system updated
  • Using strong passwords for your account

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete your account and associated data at any time by uninstalling the app
  • Access your stored information (email address) within the app
  • Revoke microphone permissions through your device settings

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes through the app or via email.

11. Third-Party Services

Our app uses your device's built-in speech recognition service (Google Speech Recognition on Android). Please refer to Google's privacy policy for information about how they handle speech data.

13. Disclaimer

Hearing Assist is an assistive tool and not a medical device. It should not be used as a replacement for professional hearing aids or medical advice.
